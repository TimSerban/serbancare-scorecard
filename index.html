The Spiritual Care & Disaster Readiness Scorecard

A structured, ten-question read on where your spiritual care program actually sits, across two dimensions most organizations have never measured.

◷ About 4 minutes ◆ 10 questions ✦ Confidential

Most spiritual care leaders are carrying real strength they have never had language for. This Scorecard gives you that language across three dimensions:

  • How mature your program is
  • How defensible it is to leadership and finance
  • How ready it is for the next crisis or care-model redesign

It measures the current, research-based Spiritual Care Maturity of your program, aligned with the past 25 years of evidence-based research in spiritual care and with the Catholic Health Association acute care department maturity levels. It also refines your team's Disaster Spiritual Care Readiness to respond to the sudden, overwhelming situations caused by local, regional, and national tragedies, and to the long-term impact on staff and colleagues.
